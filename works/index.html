RESEARCH WORKS

Classifying Images of Lung Cancer Cells using Deep Neural Network

Year: Ongoing

Description: Skin cancer is the most common form of cancer diagnosed worldwide, followed by breast cancer (among women) and prostate cancer (among men). More people all over the world die from lung cancer than any other type of cancer. This is true for both men and women. People with lung cancer are living longer after their diagnosis because more cases are found early when treatment works best. Classifying cells is important to diagnose lung cancer and the deep neural network can be used to do the task more efficiently and accurately.

Progress: Achieved Accuracy of 93.40% with Precision 0.9369, Recall 0.9340 and F1-Score 0.9338.

PROJECT WORKS

DUET Clearance Approval System

Year: 2024

Description: This is a web application that allows outgoing students, Teachers, Officers, and Staff to get clearance from and allow clearance on behalf of various Persons, Offices, Laboratories, and Sections through the Internet instead of conventionally taking/signing signatures in person. This application reduces human efforts to get clearance and saves time for both the applicant and approver.

Front End: HTML5, Tailwind CSS, Bootstrap, Javascript.

Back End: Laravel (php) and MySQL
